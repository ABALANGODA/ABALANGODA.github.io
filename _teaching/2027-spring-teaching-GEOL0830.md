**Course Overview**

- Course number: GEOL 0830: Water and Society: Sustainability, Scarcity, and Justice
- Date and Time: Mon/Wed 2-2:50
- Term: Spring
- Credits: 3

**Course Objectives**

- Analyze historical and contemporary case studies to evaluate how water management decisions influence ecological systems and human communities across different regions and scales.
- Evaluate a range of water management strategies and propose practical, evidence-based solutions that account for environmental, social, and economic tradeoffs in real-world contexts.
- Reflect on the ethical, cultural, and societal dimensions of water use and management in the context of global environmental change and sustainability challenges.
